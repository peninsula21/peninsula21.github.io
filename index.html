Peninsula21 School

Coming soon to Mount Martha, Mornington Peninsula — where students master the digital world through hands-on STEM education, blending the Victorian Digital Technologies Curriculum with critical thinking to develop the next generation of tech innovators.

The Peninsula21 Difference

Our Vision

To cultivate a generation of tech innovators, equipped with the digital fluency and critical thinking skills to lead in a rapidly evolving world.

We are building an innovative approach to education that will foster creativity and critical thinking in every student.

Our Mission

Peninsula21 will provide a nurturing environment where each child can thrive academically and personally within a rigorous STEM framework.

We will blend the Victorian Curriculum with advanced digital technologies, fostering collaborative problem-solving from day one.

Peninsula21's Digital Curriculum

Excellence through innovation, coding, and logical systems thinking

💻

Digital Systems

Students will examine the core components of digital systems and networks. From understanding hardware to how systems transmit data, we will build foundational tech knowledge.

📊

Data & Information

Our focus will be on acquiring, storing, and validating data. Students will learn to interpret and visualise information while maintaining ethical and social protocols in online collaboration.

🚀

Creating Digital Solutions

We will empower students to define problems, design user interfaces, and build visual and text-based algorithms. Critical branching and iteration skills will form our core coding approach.

How Peninsula21 Will Foster Innovators

Standards-Based STEM Learning

Our curriculum will deliver the full Victorian K-6 Digital Technologies curriculum, ensuring deep understanding of data representation, networks, and problem-solving strategies.

Students will progress through highly structured algorithmic thinking pathways that provide flexibility to excel well beyond grade-level expectations in computer science.

Hands-on Tech Environment

Industry-experienced educators will guide students through real-world tech projects, fostering growth mindsets and helping them build strong algorithmic habits.

Our campus will combine modern makerspaces, robotics gear, and software tools to create an environment where every future engineer or developer can thrive.

Core Algorithmic Excellence

Students will engage rigorously with core sequences, branching, and iteration. We will teach both visual blocks (like Scratch) and typed languages suitable for senior levels.

From day one, we are committed to building a culture of respect, incorporating strong digital citizenship and cybersecurity fundamentals.

Sustainability & Ethics

Developing digital solutions isn't just about code. We will emphasise how systems meet current and future community needs while evaluating sustainability impacts.

We will intentionally integrate digital tools to provide rich, interactive experiences, ensuring a healthy balance with offline learning opportunities.

A Specialised STEM Approach

Dedicated tech educators building future leaders

Bridging Code and Community

Peninsula21 employs fully qualified tech-focused teachers who leverage advanced systems to ensure world-class conceptual understanding of programming and digital ecosystems.

Our educators teach students to define complex problems, decompose them logically, and synthesise working algorithms via continuous testing and iteration.

Our comprehensive K-6 programme includes: Algorithm design and implementation • Data visualisation • Cybersecurity fundamentals • Robotics and physical computing • Social and ecological tech impacts
