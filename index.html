How Peninsula21 Works

Personalised Learning at the Right Pace

Our AI-powered learning platform provides each student with 1:1 personalised education, delivering coursework at their individual pace and appropriate level.

Students progress with concept-based mastery, ensuring no knowledge gaps while accelerating through content they grasp quickly.

Motivation Through Purpose

We motivate students by giving them time back to pursue what they're passionate about and develop essential life skills.

Expert guides support motivated learners, fostering growth mindsets and cultivating independent, self-directed learning habits.

Morning: 2-Hour Academic Mastery

Students complete their core academic learning in just 2 hours each morning using adaptive technology that provides truly personalised education.

This efficient approach means students accomplish more in less time, without the frustration of being held back or the stress of falling behind.

Afternoon: Real-World Skills

With academics complete, students spend afternoons developing life skills through hands-on workshops covering entrepreneurship, creative arts, coding, outdoor education, and more.

They explore interests, work on passion projects, and gain practical skills that prepare them for the real world.

The Peninsula21 Approach

Reimagining the role of educators

Expert Guides, Not Traditional Teachers

At Peninsula21, educators shift from traditional roles of lecturing and grading to becoming mentors and guides who support students' emotional growth, motivation, and life skill development.

This transformation allows our guides to focus on what matters most: inspiring students, fostering self-driven learning, and teaching critical real-world skills that will serve them for life.

Our guides provide: Motivational and emotional support • Guidance on AI-powered learning • Life skills instruction • Passion project mentorship
